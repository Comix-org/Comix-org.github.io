Privacy Policy

Honestly, we do not store any of your data. There is no need we would need it. We do not use cookies, either.
The only data we take from you is the comics you favorited, which is stored locally on your computer in localStorage. So whatever comics you favorite are stored locally on your computer, and there is no way we can access it.
